The Start

The three you see above are the best stories I have witnessed in my entire life, and I believe anybody who watches them would feel the same.

I used to belittle my friends, saying that anime was for kids, joking about it, and never really even looking into it.

One day, we bunked our class and were roaming around in our college when I saw my friend watching Attack on Titan. I was really intrigued after watching only 10 minutes of the episode.

And that was only the start. I watched all three seasons of the show in 3 days and even went on to read the entire manga, following all the new chapters as they were released until the very end.

I then started watching other popular anime as well, like Naruto and Demon Slayer. Around this time, I got into Manhwa, and that led me to Solo Leveling and Nano Machine.

After watching One Piece, I had a serious of adrenaline rush, and then I had all the manuscripts until then destroyed and restarted the whole story.

The reason I started this story? One day, one of my friends encouraged me to explore new ways to keep myself occupied, and that was it.

I started the initial manuscript in March 2023 and began working on this website in September 2024. Hopefully, I want to publish this to the public in 2025 and be active on social media as part of my studio.
