Dictionary

Got any questions regarding structuring of the story?

You should be able to find the answers here.

Protagonist Arc

The emotional and narrative journey of the main character throughout the story.

Inciting Incident

The event that disrupts the normal world and sets the story in motion.

Foreshadowing

A literary device that hints at future events to build anticipation or tension.

Climax

The moment of greatest conflict or emotional intensity in the story.

Resolution

The conclusion where remaining plot threads are tied up and balance is restored.

Worldbuilding

The process of constructing the setting, cultures, and rules of the fictional universe.
