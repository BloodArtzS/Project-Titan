My Inspiration

1

One Piece


One Piece is a long-running anime and manga series created by Eiichiro Oda, following the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate who dreams of becoming the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure known as One Piece, left behind by the previous Pirate King, Gol D. Roger.

2

Nano Machine

Nano Machine is a manhwa that follows the story of Cheon Yeo-Woon, an orphan who is not fully accepted by his own people. After being held in disdain and having his life put in danger, Cheon Yeo-Woon receives an unexpected visit from his descendant from the future who injects a nano machine into his body, drastically changing his life after its activation.

3

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is an anime series that follows the story of humanity's struggle against giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The series is set in a world where humanity lives behind three massive walls to protect themselves from these Titans, who are immune to almost all forms of attack except for a vulnerable spot at the nape of their necks.

The Start

The three you see above are the best stories I have witnessed in my entire life, and I believe anybody who watches them would feel the same.

I used to belittle my friends, saying that anime was for kids, joking about it, and never really even looking into it.

One day, we bunked our class and were roaming around in our college when I saw my friend watching Attack on Titan. I was really intrigued after watching only 10 minutes of the episode.

And that was only the start. I watched all three seasons of the show in 3 days and even went on to read the entire manga, following all the new chapters as they were released until the very end.

I then started watching other popular anime as well, like Naruto and Demon Slayer. Around this time, I got into Manhwa, and that led me to Solo Leveling and Nano Machine.
