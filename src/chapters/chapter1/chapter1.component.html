Pre-story

The world was submerged in the ocean in 2097 and Among the 15.6 Billion - only 16.3 million of the world's population survived. The world has changed a lot since the great flood of 2097. After the catastrophe, humanity had to adapt to new ways of living, and one of the most significant changes was the construction of Hydronova, the largest fully equipped battleship city ever built. The Hydronova is a marvel of modern engineering,

Currently in 2205 - Present!
