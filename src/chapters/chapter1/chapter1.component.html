Opening Chapter 1

Currently in 2205

Total 4 Mega stations in the world at the moment. You see all the planes flying around to celebrate the 155th anniversary after the world sank. Almost everyone has a happy face, except for the few old geezers who know how bad the catastrophe was back then in time because they had experienced it themselves.

As celebrations were in full swing, we could see a group of 12-14 people starting to surround the Mega station from around.

Agent 1: Man, I wish I could take the time off to enjoy the view of celebrations.

Agent 2: I feel you, but it is our responsibility to keep the insides safe from the deep sea dangers.

Agent 1: I know that, but still… nevermind.

The view for the sector 33 camera feed gets cut off.

Agent 2: Betsy, look at this, the feed got cut off just now.

Agent 1: What! Are you kidding? Those cameras are guarded by a bullet-proof shield, even your most talented Titan will have a hard time trying to break it, even if someone tries to break it by force, we would see them in the recorded feed. Check the previous feed before it was cut off.

Agent 2: I was checking the same while you were nagging me, but I didn’t see anyone come near to the base from the below.

Betsy: This sounds like trouble for us, Andre. Let's go out and check if we can see something through our Radeon Glasses.

Betsy and Andre came out of the Guard Station, they were looking down at the exact spot, but they couldn’t see anything. Suddenly they feel a presence in front of them, and before they can even lift their head, their heads get chopped off.

As their heads hit the surface, you can see a person landing beside their dead bodies.
